PROJECTS

SimClimate is an educational video game made in Unreal Engine 5 that explores the relationship between humanity's desire to always grow, and the reality of living in a closed ecosystem with a finite amount of resources.
The Orbiter is a video game made in Unity that teaches players about Newton's Universal Law of Gravity. Try to bring the rocket home while discovering the effects of mass and distance on gravity.
